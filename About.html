About the Tournament

This Tournament is being organized by government of Jeonju City. There will be 16 teams playing and the tournamet will be organised as a league. There will be money prizes also.

Prizes:

  1. 1.000.000 KRW for winner team
  2. 700.000 KRW for second
  3. 500.000 KRW for third

Note: Make sure to wear only futsal shoes during Tournament
